--- layout: home search_exclude: true image: images/logo.png --- Rachel Thomas is founding director of the [Center for Applied Data Ethics (CADE)](https://www.usfca.edu/data-institute/initiatives/center-applied-data-ethics) at the University of San Francisco, where she researches risks and harms of algorithmic systems. She is also co-founder of [fast.ai](https://course.fast.ai/), helping people from diverse and non-standard backgrounds learn state-of-the-art AI. She earned her PhD in mathematics. Rachel has taught courses on [data ethics](https://ethics.fast.ai/), [natural language processing](https://www.fast.ai/2019/07/08/fastai-nlp/), and [computational linear algebra](https://www.fast.ai/2017/07/17/num-lin-alg/). ![](/images/2020_08_29_Thomas_1868.jpg) *Photo by Gabriela Hasbun*
